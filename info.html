About the App

Expense Manager helps you track your daily expenses, manage your income, and understand your spending habits. The app is designed to be simple, fast, and secure.

Key Features

  • Create custom categories
  • View monthly spending reports
  • Budget management
  • Clean and easy to use interface
  • Local data storage for privacy
