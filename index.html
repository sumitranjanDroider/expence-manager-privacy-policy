Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Expance Manager ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

The Expense Manager app is designed to help users track their personal finances. Most of the data entered into the app such as expenses, income, categories, and notes is stored locally on the user's device.

We do not collect or store your financial data on our servers.

The app may collect limited technical information automatically such as:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Application performance data
  • Crash logs

How Your Information Is Used

The information collected may be used to:

  • Improve application performance
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Enhance the user experience

Financial Data

All financial information such as expenses, income records, and categories entered by the user remain on the device unless the user explicitly chooses to export or back up the data.

We do not access, collect, or share your financial records.

Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services that may collect anonymous information for analytics and performance monitoring. These services may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Crash reporting tools

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no electronic storage method is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

This application is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
